About Me

I am a Full Stack Developer, and Full Time Tinkerer. Tech News is my lifeblood, and I love following the newest advances in every field of technology. When I'm not working on a project, Im usually dreaming up a new one.
I went to school for IT at Whatcom Community College, and started at ServiceMonster as a technical support rep in December 2015. Since then I have worked my way up, and am now a full stack developer, still at ServiceMonster. In that time I taught myself Python, Javascript, and enough C# to be dangerous. I also still keep the computers running and the packets flowing at the office.
My free time right now consists mostly of either playing Dungeons & Dragons, working on my D&D campaigns (minis, terrain, and story), cooking, and of course games. I'm a big WoW nerd, especially Vanilla, and will happily reminisce with you about the "good old days".
If you think I might be a good fit at your company, I'd love to hear from you! I am always open to new opportunities.
